124 october, 1773. same day a sweepstakes of 500gs each, h. st b. c. was won by lord grosvenor's mambrino — 1 lord clermont's marc anthony, 8st. — 2 mr. vernon's florizel, 8st. 6lb. p. ft. — four to 1 on mambrino. same day mr. scawen's alexis, 3 years old, 7st. 4lb. beat lord clermont's pulpé, 4 years old, 8st. 4 lb. ditch in, 200gs.— three to 1 on pulpé. same day mr. walker's hibernian, 8st. beat mr. strode's cicero, 8st. 7lb. r. m. for 100gs. — two to 1 on cicero. same day lord clermont's johnny, 7st. 7lb. beat mr. brand's don carlos, 8st. b. c. 200gs. — six to 5 on johnny. same day mr. scawen's mecea, 8st. paid 25gs compromise to lord clermont's squirrella, 7st. 10lb. across the flat, 100gs, h. ft. same day lord clermont's hercules, 7st. 9lb. paid 100gs compromise to mr. ogilvy's consul, 8st. 7lb. ditch in. — lord clermont to have staked 6oogs to 400. same day lord ossory's chalkstone, 7st. 11lb. paid forfeit to lord grosvenor's sweet william, 8st. 10lb. b. c. 200gs. h. ft. on the 20th the 140gs for 6 year olds, carrying 8st. 10lb. and aged 9st. 2lb. the sole property of subscribers, b. c. was won by lord bolingbroke's marianne, 6 years old — 1 lord ossory's augur, 5 years old — 2 at starting, 6 to 4 on marianne; in running, 6 to 1 on augur. same
