(chiefly when any eminent conjunction or aspect falls in with them) do not certainly induce moisture? i beseech you (my honoured and worthy friends) to take some notice of these matters, which you may easily do, by keeping a diary of the weather; as also of the effects of your vernal ingresses, (which i intend, god permitting me life, once a year to present you withal;) and pray ye also observe, together therewith, the transits, conjunctions, &c. of the planets over the prime parts of your islands palladium, or genesius; that they may be preserved by your generous communication, as well for the advance of truth in science, as advantage of your selves and country. thus, worthy friends and countrymen, wishing you all happiness, and, as so many successful 'columbus's, to increase as well in your acquaintance with the influences of the glorious diamonds of heaven, as in the gold and treasure of the indies;
