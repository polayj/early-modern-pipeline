(824) — primo avulso non deficit alter aureus, & famili frondescit virga metallo. the whole complex of ingredients is known to consist only of vulgar aqua fortis (abstracted from two parts of vitriol, and one of salt-peter) and quick-silver, and a small quantity of silver, far less than you may reap in a small time from those silver-sprigs; yet gain there is none, there being more expences blown away into smoak by continuance of fire in one month, than can be recover’d from this silver-harvest in a longer time. and though this seem but a toy, yet ’tis very philosophical, much informing the understanding, however it enrich not the purfe. for here we see crude mercury manifestly turn’d into silver, notwithstanding ’tis deny’d by so many. so far this generous observer for this time;
