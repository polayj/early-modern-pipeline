cordials, if he chance to vomit it up again, then wash his mouth with rosewater and vinegar, and then give him more of the same again, which must be proportioned according to the quantity vomited, for if all were vomited, then give so much more: (if lese) then according to the quantity vomited, and if he vomit that also, then give him more, and so continue it.
